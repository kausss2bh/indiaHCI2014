Sponsorship

The three day conference is attracting eminent delegates from across the globe. More than 200 delegates from various fields of research related to Human Computer Interaction will be attending the conference.

It is a good opportunity for brands to make their presence felt by becoming a sponsor for the event.

The conference team has identified some ways in which a sponsoring agency can mark their presence.


Name of sponsorship Amount in INR No. of Sponsors Promises from Conference Team
Title Sponsorship 5 Lakhs 1 Display of brand logo wherever the conference name is mentioned in any format including website, main entrance banner, backdrop banner, brochures, delegate kit etc. , provision for 10ft x 10ft central stall space for branding and display of the company, play of 5 min. video from the sponsoring agency in the main conference venue.
Conference Lunch 1 Lakh 2 Branding at the lunch venue, public announcement in the main conference venue, video display of company promotion at lunch venue
Stationary 1 Lakh 1 Branding on the stationary shared with delegates of the conference.
Hospitality 2 Lakhs 1 Brand display on conference website as hospitality partner for conference and branding at the keynote speakers venue for all the 4 speakers.
Gala Dinner 2 Lakhs 1 Branding at the dinner venue, public announcement in the main conference venue, video display of company promotion at dinner venue
Stalls 1 Lakh 10 6 ft x 4ft space for stalls in a covered area with permission to display products or branding messages without any sound.
Transportation 1 Lakh 1 Display on website as travel partner and provide Kiosk space for travel booking of delegates at the conference venue where booking for site seeing in and around Delhi etc. can be facilitated.
T-Shirt 1 Lakh 1 Display on website as travel partner and provide Kiosk space for travel booking of delegates at the conference venue where booking for site seeing in and around Delhi etc. can be facilitated.
Unconference 2 Lakhs 2 This event will involve semi-structured and unstructured discussions on the topics related to HCI in the evenings between 5pm and 9pm after the main conference is over. The sponsoring agency's will be provided a promotion stall of 10"*10" in the venue where they can play promotional videos and display other promotional materials. Name of the sponsoring agency will be announced in the unconference events.

All contributions are to be made in advance before November 5th 2014 for the conference team to be able to deliver its promises.
