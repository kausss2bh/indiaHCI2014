Unconference

Apart from the workshops, research paper presentations and panel discussions, IndiaHCI 2014 is also organising ‘unconference’, a series of events, which will allow the conference participants to share their views in semi-structured discussions. Some of the identified events of unconference that you can plan to participate are:


Pecha Kucha on “UX for sustainable India” 7th Dec

Pecha Kucha in Japanese means “chit chat”. This form of presentation helps one express views and ideas in a short and crisp way. IndiaHCI 2014 gives you the opportunity to present your ideas on Petcha Kucha evening which will be held on 7th Dec, 2014 at 6pm onwards in IIT Delhi Lawns. The topic is “UX for sustainable India”. If you wish to be one of the presenters, below are the guidelines for you:

Guidelines:
  • 20 presenters
  • 20 slides per presenter
  • 20 seconds per slide
  • Keep it short and crisp
  • Make it interesting
  • Each slide can contain text/images but No animation, video or music
  • When you are introduced on stage, open your presentation, press start and away you go. The slides will change automatically every 20 seconds; you won’t need to press anything.
  • Pecha Kucha works best if you have rehearsed beforehand.

If you feel like participating, you can prepare your 20 slides and send the deck to Unconference@indiahci2014.in with cc to GeneralChair@indiahci2014.in . Please name your deck as “your name _Pecha Kucha”. The selection of presenters will be on the basis of quality of slides and first come first serve. So if you submit quality ppt slides early enough, you have a chance of getting selected to participate. All selected presenters will be required to register for the conference.


Design Lies on “Design for rural India” 8th Dec

Present a design lie to the indiaHCI 2014 audience, an IMPOSSIBLE design idea on topic of “Design for Rural India”. Selected design ideas will be invited to present in the unconference.

Guidelines:
  • Think STUPID
  • 1 idea
  • 10 slides
  • Create self running slides: Maximum time – 10 minutes.
  • Make it interesting
  • Make the audience curious and make them think
  • Do not use videos. Use only text/images in MS Powerpoint.

If you feel like participating, you can prepare your 10 slides and send the deck to Unconference@indiahci2014.in with cc to GeneralChair@indiahci2014.in . Please name your deck as “ your name_Design Lies”. The selection of presenters will be on the basis of quality of slides and first come first serve. So if you submit quality ppt slides early enough, you have a chance of getting selected to participate. All selected presenters will be required to register for the conference.


Knowledge Café on “HCI in Education” 8th Dec

A knowledge café is a type of group discussion workshop which aims to provide an open and creative conversation on a topic of interest to surface their collective knowledge, share ideas and insights, and gain a deeper understanding of the subject and issues involved. Topic for the evening of 8th December – “HCI in Education”.

Guidelines:
  • Send your stand/perspective on HCI in education in about 200 words to express your interest in a MS Word file with Times New Roman font and font size twelve by 6 pm, 20th November, 2014 to Unconference@indiahci2014.in with cc to GeneralChair@indiahci2014.in . Please name your file as “ your name_Knowledge Cafe”.
  • If your topic is selected, you will be informed beforehand and you will be invited to participate in the knowledge café.
  • All participants are required to be registered for the conference.
