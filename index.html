    Expanding Visions!

    After a series of successful national conference for the past 5 years, this year we are holding the prestigious “India HCI 2014” at IIT Delhi during 8th and 9th December, 2014. The theme of the conference, 'Expanding Visions' aims to capture the current transition from computers at the desk to all kinds of handheld computing devices, ably amplified by the Internet. Needless to say, the impact has been pervasive in all aspects of human life. The conference will deliberate the processes and prognosis of the field, Human Computer Interaction (HCI) from variety of multiple disciplines! India HCI 2014 will provide an excellent platform for exchange of thoughts and ideas between researchers of HCI domain. Apart from keynote lectures by eminent researchers and practitioners in the field, there will be tutorials, workshops and paper presentations on all the possible topics of HCI under the theme, Expanding Visions! Contributions are invited from academics and practitioners of HCI who can be from computer science, computer graphics, communication studies, design, psychology, sociology, philosophy, and information systems etc. The conference will also provide space for showcase their technologies, products and services to the delegates!
