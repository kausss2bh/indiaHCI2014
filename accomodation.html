How to Reach?

From the Airport

IGI Airport is about 10 kilometres from the IIT Delhi campus. A pre-paid taxi or a radio-taxi (such as Merucab, Easycab, etc.) can be taken from the airport. Typical fare is about ₹500 - ₹600.

From Railway Stations

The Institute campus is 19 kilometres from Old Delhi Railway Station, 14 kilometres from New Delhi Railway Station and 12 kilometres from Hazrat Nizamuddin Railway Station. Pre-paid auto-rickshaws are available at railway stations. From Old Delhi and New Delhi stations, the most convenient transport is the Delhi Metro. Take the Yellow line of Delhi Metro from Chandni Chowk Metro Station (near Old Delhi Railway Station) or New Delhi Metro Station going towards Huda City Center runs via Hauz Khas Metro Station, which is about 1 kilometre from IIT main gate. From the Metro station one can take an autorichshaw to IIT Campus; the typical fare is ₹50 - ₹60. More information on Delhi Metro is available at Delhi Metro Website.

About Delhi

Delhi is a city that bridges two different worlds. Old Delhi, once the capital of Islamic India, is a labyrinth of narrow lanes lined with crumbling havelis and formidable mosques. In contrast, the imperial city of New Delhi created by the British Raj is composed of spacious, tree-lined avenues and imposing government buildings. In Delhi, you will discover that the city is sprinkled with dazzling gems: captivating ancient monuments, fascinating museums and art galleries, architectural wonders, a vivacious performing-arts scene, fabulous eating places and bustling markets.

Delhi Weather

In December, Delhi could be fairly cold. Typical temperatures range from 20 to 25 C (in the daytime) to 12 to 15 C (in the night). Woollen clothes are advisable even during the day time. Often Delhi weather could be foggy/misty in the mornings and evenings.

Nearby Delhi

Agra, housing the world famous Taj Mahal is around 200 Kms (~3 hours drive) from Delhi. One day trip from Delhi to Agra and back is being organized by various travels and tours.

Jaipur, also called the Pink city, that houses beautiful palaces and Amber fort is around 250 Kms (~4 hours drive) from Delhi. One day trip from Delhi to Jaipur is being organized by various travels and tours.
