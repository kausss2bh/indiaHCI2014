Call for Student Volunteers


The 6th India HCI Conference is now open for Student Volunteer registrations. The conference will be held in IIT Delhi, India, from 7th to 9th of December 2014. See www.indiahci2014.in for more details on the conference.

The last date of application for Student Volunteer Registration is 10th September 2014.

Register Now


Benefits for Student Volunteers

  • Free India HCI 2014 Conference Registration (worth Rupees 8,000).
  • Free registration to a Workshop of your choice.
  • Opportunity to network with professionals, academicians and students working in the field of HCI in India.
  • Get to work and collaborate with student organizers from across the country.


Frequently Asked Questions


Q1. What will I do when I become a Student Volunteer?

By becoming a student volunteer, you become a member of the organizing team of India HCI 2014. You are required to carry the following tasks-

Pre-Event:
  • The volunteers will be our ambassadors at their respective institutes, their roles primarily includes promoting the conference, holding sessions at their campuses etc.
  • Help in content creation and management.
  • Indoor/Outdoor management
During event:
  • Work at least 20 hours during INDIA HCI 2014 Conference week (7th to 9th Dec 2014).
  • Student volunteers are needed to help with providing support during conference/workshop/tutorial sessions, conference proceedings documentation, set-up/break-down, registration etc.
  • The volunteers will have to collaborate and work towards conference space design, includes setting up cool installations at conference venue.

Q2. Who is eligible to become a Student Volunteer?

You are eligible if you meet any of the following criteria:

  • Enrolled students: You should be undergraduate, graduate or research student enrolled full time for at least one semester during academic year 2013-2014
  • Will get Enrolled: You will be joining as a full time student before 7th December 2014. You will need to produce your Student ID cards at the time of the conference.


Q3. How to apply?

All those interested in volunteering for INDIA HCI 2014 need to register here.

You will need to provide your personal and academic details to complete the registration. The selection is a 2-step process; after registration, our coordinators will reach out to you through email/phone and will continue with the process. 20 students will be selected on the basis of skills, expertise and academic origin. This is just to take care that we have enough variety among volunteers. You will be notified about the selection results by 15th September 2014.


Q4. What is the deadline?

The last date of application for Student Volunteer Registration is 10th September 2014 (23:59 IST).
